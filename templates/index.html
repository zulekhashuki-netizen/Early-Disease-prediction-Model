Applications

Diagnosis Tools

This application uses patient symptoms and medical history to estimate possible diseases. It helps in improving the accuracy and speed of medical diagnosis.

Symptom Analysis

It identifies common patterns and combinations of symptoms that are linked to specific diseases in the dataset.

Risk Profiling

It evaluates how factors such as age, gender, blood pressure, and cholesterol influence the likelihood of developing certain diseases.

AI Training

The dataset is used to train machine learning models that learn the relationship between patient profiles and disease outcomes for prediction purposes.

Healthcare Management

It supports planning and management of hospital resources by analyzing patient trends and predicted health conditions.

Medical Research

It assists in studying disease patterns and relationships within patient data to support medical research and improve healthcare understanding.
